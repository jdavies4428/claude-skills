## KEY STATS & FINDINGS SUMMARY

### Platform Performance (45+ Demographic)
- Facebook dominance: 65+ users choose it over other platforms
- Reels optimal: 60-90 second vertical videos get highest engagement
- Audio critical: Video WITH sound = 34.5% lower CPA than silent
- Watch time: Older adults watch longer and replay emotional moments
- Share rate: 3-6% on emotional content (vs. 0.5% average)

### Emotional Triggers (Research-Backed)
1. Direct eye contact in restored photo
2. Seeing a beloved face clearly again
3. Handwritten context (names, dates, relationships)
4. "Before it's too late" messaging paired with hope
5. Generational connection (past → future)
6. Reunion metaphor language
7. Tactile, reverent handling (albums, textures)
8. Light and warmth imagery

### The 8 Concepts (Ranked by Impact)
**Highest Emotional Impact:**
- Concept 4: The Face Returns (eyes-first restoration)
- Concept 8: Face to Face with the Past (intimate zoom)

**Highest Conversion Potential:**
- Concept 2: Legacy Unlocked (preservation urgency)
- Concept 3: Through the Generations (generational message)

**Highest Universal Appeal:**
- Concept 1: The Memory Returns (contemplative, accessible)
- Concept 6: The Warmth Returns (life-affirming, joyful)

**Specialized Use Cases:**
- Concept 5: Dust of Time (poetic, philosophical)
- Concept 7: Opening the Album (archive, tactile)

### Expected Performance
**Conservative:** $25 CPA, 45% completion rate
**Expected:** $15 CPA, 55% completion rate
**Optimistic:** $8 CPA, 70% completion rate

(Emotional content typically outperforms by 40-60%)

## QUICK ANSWERS TO COMMON QUESTIONS

**Q: Which concept should we start with?**
A: Concepts 1, 4, or 2. Start with 1 (universal appeal) or 4 (highest emotional impact).

**Q: How long should videos be?**
A: 60-90 seconds, with 75 seconds optimal for 45+ demographic.

**Q: Do we need sound?**
A: Yes. Video WITH sound = 34.5% lower CPA. Audio is critical.

**Q: What music style works best?**
A: Piano, music box, cello, soft strings. Anything familiar and acoustic (not trendy beats).

**Q: Should we use urgency in copy?**
A: No. Legacy language ("Preserve," "Protect") outperforms urgency.

**Q: Which audience buys most?**
A: Women 45-54 have highest click-through rates; 50-75+ most likely to share/replay.

**Q: What's the expected CAC?**
A: Conservative $800-2,000; Expected $400-800; Optimistic $200-400.

**Q: What's the most important metric?**
A: Watch-through rate and share rate (emotional content multiplier).

**Q: How many concepts should we run simultaneously?**
A: Start with 2-3 in Phase 1; expand to all 8 by Phase 3.

**Q: What's the biggest mistake to avoid?**
A: Fast pacing. The #1 error is rushing emotional moments. Let them breathe.
